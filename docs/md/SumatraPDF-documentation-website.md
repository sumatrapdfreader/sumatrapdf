[SumatraPDF](https://www.sumatrapdfreader.org/) is a free [PDF, EPUB, MOBI, CHM, XPS, DjVu, CBZ and CBR](Supported-document-formats.md) reader for Windows. It's small, fast, customizable and full of features.
